About

Hello! I’m Isabella, a fourth-year Electrical and Computer Engineering student at the University of Texas at Austin.

I’ve built a strong foundation in semiconductors, hardware, software development, and robotics through internships at AMD and Texas Instruments, undergraduate research in robotics, and hands-on projects.

My passion lies in computing systems and semiconductors, with a particular focus on computer architecture, digital design, and hardware development.

I enjoy building systems from the ground up, solving complex problems, and collaborating on projects that integrate hardware and software in meaningful ways. If you're interested in connecting or working together, feel free to reach out!
